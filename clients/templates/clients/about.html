About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Modern Carwash has been dedicated to providing exceptional car care services to the Nairobi community. What started as a small operation has grown into a trusted name in automotive care.

We believe that every vehicle deserves the best care possible. That's why we've invested in state-of-the-art equipment, eco-friendly products, and a team of passionate professionals who treat every car as if it were their own.

Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for thousands of car owners across Kenya.
